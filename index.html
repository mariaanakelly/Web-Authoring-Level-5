Live Life to the Full

"To insure good health: eat lightly, breathe deeply, live moderately, cultivate cheerfulness and maintain an interest in life.”  ~William Londen

A nutritious, well-balanced diet along with exercise is the foundation for healthy living. Healthy eating includes consuming food from the different food groups

Research shows that excessive consumption of foods high in fats, salt and sugar, as well as low levels of vegetables and fruit intake, play a significant role in increasing our risk of developing chronic diseases.
